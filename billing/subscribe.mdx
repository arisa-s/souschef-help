You can subscribe to [Souschef Plus](/billing/subscription-benefits) directly inside the app — no separate account or external website needed. The whole process happens through the App Store on iPhone or Google Play on Android, so your payment details stay safely with Apple or Google.

## Souschef Plus pricing

- **\$2.99 USD/month**
- **\$24.99 USD/year**

Both plans include unlimited recipe imports and ad-free browsing and importing.

## How to subscribe

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iPhone (iOS)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Souschef">
        Open the **Souschef** app on your iPhone and go to **Settings** or **Account**, then tap the option to upgrade to **Souschef Plus**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose a plan">
        Pick either the **\$2.99/month** or **\$24.99/year** plan.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm your purchase">
        Review the pricing and billing details, then tap **Subscribe**. You may be asked to confirm with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.
      </Step>
      <Step title="You're all set">
        Your Souschef Plus subscription is now active. Unlimited imports and ad-free browsing take effect right away.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Souschef">
        Open the **Souschef** app on your Android phone and go to **Settings** or **Account**, then tap the option to upgrade to **Souschef Plus**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose a plan">
        Pick either the **\$2.99/month** or **\$24.99/year** plan.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm your purchase">
        Review the pricing details and tap **Subscribe**. Confirm the payment using your Google account billing method.
      </Step>
      <Step title="You're all set">
        Your Souschef Plus subscription activates right away. Unlimited imports and ad-free browsing are available immediately.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple or Google — Souschef does not process payments directly. If you need to update your payment method or view billing history, you'll do that through your Apple ID or Google account settings.
</Note>
