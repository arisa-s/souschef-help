When a Souschef subscription is available, you'll be able to sign up directly through your phone's app store — no separate account or external website needed. The whole process happens inside the App Store on iPhone or Google Play on Android, so your payment details stay safely with Apple or Google.

## How to subscribe

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iPhone (iOS)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the App Store">
        On your iPhone, open the **App Store** and search for **Souschef**, or
        tap the link from within the app itself.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Find the subscription option">
        On the Souschef app page, scroll down to find the subscription options.
        Tap the plan you'd like to subscribe to.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm your purchase">
        Review the pricing and billing details, then tap **Subscribe**. You may
        be asked to confirm with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.
      </Step>
      <Step title="You're all set!">
        Your subscription is now active. Open Souschef and your premium features
        will be unlocked automatically.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Google Play">
        On your Android phone, open the **Google Play** app and search for
        **Souschef**, or follow the in-app link to the store listing.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Find the subscription option">
        On the Souschef app page, scroll down to find the available subscription
        plans and tap the one you'd like.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm your purchase">
        Review the pricing details and tap **Subscribe**. Confirm the payment
        using your Google account billing method.
      </Step>
      <Step title="You're all set!">
        Your subscription will activate immediately. Open Souschef and your
        premium features will be ready to use.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple or Google — Souschef does not
  process payments directly. If you need to update your payment method or view
  billing history, you'll do that through your Apple ID or Google account
  settings.
</Note>
