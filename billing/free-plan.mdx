Souschef is free to download from the App Store and Google Play — no credit card, no sign-up fee. The free plan lets you use the app's core features, with a limit on how many recipes you can import each week and ads shown while browsing and importing. If you'd like unlimited imports and ad-free browsing, [Souschef Plus](/billing/subscription-benefits) is available for a small monthly or yearly cost.

## What's included on the free plan

- **Up to 5 recipe imports per week** from any [supported source](/importing/supported-sources) — Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube, Pinterest, recipe websites, photos, and pasted text
- **Ads shown while browsing and importing** — ads help keep the free plan available
- All the core cooking features — [Cooking Mode](/using/cooking-mode), [smart timers](/using/timers), [serving adjustment](/using/adjust-servings), [measurement conversion](/using/convert-measurements), [shopping lists](/using/shopping-list), and the [prep checklist](/using/prep-checklist)

<Info>
  Free users can import up to **5 recipes per week**. Your weekly count resets on a rolling basis. Once you've used your 5 imports, you can either wait for the next reset or upgrade to [Souschef Plus](/billing/subscription-benefits) for unlimited imports.
</Info>

## Free vs. Souschef Plus

|  | Free | Souschef Plus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Recipe imports | 5 per week | Unlimited |
| Ads while browsing and importing | Shown | Removed |

Souschef is an independently built app. Souschef Plus helps cover the cost of running recipe imports, keeps the free plan available for everyone, and supports ongoing improvements. Every Souschef Plus subscription helps keep recipe imports reliable, supports free users, and allows the app to continue improving.

<Tip>
  Download Souschef on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/souschef-recipe-organizer/id6468939420) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.souschef.app) — no credit card required.
</Tip>
