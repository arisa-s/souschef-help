Souschef is completely free to download from the App Store and Google Play — no credit card, no sign-up fee, nothing. Right now, every feature in the app is available to all users at no cost. That means you can dive straight into saving recipes, cooking with confidence, and building your grocery lists without worrying about hitting a paywall.

## What's included for free

Here's everything you get with the free version of Souschef today:

- **Recipe import** — Pull in recipes from almost any recipe blog, as well as YouTube, Pinterest, Instagram, and TikTok
- **Cooking Mode** — A distraction-free, step-by-step view designed to keep your place while you cook
- **Smart timers** — Set timers directly from recipe steps so you never lose track of what's in the oven
- **Serving adjustment** — Scale any recipe up or down to fit however many people you're feeding
- **Measurement conversion** — Switch between cups, grams, ounces, and more without doing the math yourself
- **Shopping lists** — Build and manage grocery lists straight from your saved recipes
- **Prep checklist** — Check off ingredients and steps as you go so nothing gets missed

<Info>
  All features are free at this time. A premium subscription may be introduced in the future — we'll give you plenty of notice before anything changes.
</Info>

  Download Souschef free on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com) — no credit card required.
