Souschef may introduce a paid subscription in the future to unlock additional features and help support the continued development of the app. When that happens, a subscription is designed to give you even more tools to make cooking at home easier, more organised, and more enjoyable — while keeping the core Souschef experience available to everyone.

## How subscriptions work

When a Souschef subscription becomes available, it will be managed entirely through your phone's app store — not through Souschef directly. This means:

- **iPhone users** subscribe and manage their plan through the **Apple App Store**
- **Android users** subscribe and manage their plan through **Google Play**

Your billing, payment method, and renewal settings will all live inside your Apple or Google account, just like any other app subscription you might already have.

## Why a subscription?

Building and maintaining a great app takes time and resources. A subscription option allows the Souschef team to keep improving the app, add new features, and provide better support — while making sure anyone who just wants the core experience can still use it for free.

We'll always be upfront with you about what's changing and what it costs before anything shifts.
