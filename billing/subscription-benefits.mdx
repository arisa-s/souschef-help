**Souschef Plus** is the paid subscription that unlocks the full Souschef experience. Free users can import up to 5 recipes per week; Souschef Plus removes that limit and takes the ads out of browsing and importing.

Souschef is an independently built app. Souschef Plus helps cover the cost of running recipe imports, keeps the free plan available for everyone, and supports ongoing improvements.

## What's included with Souschef Plus

- **Unlimited recipe imports** — no weekly cap
- **Ad-free browsing and importing** — no ads anywhere in the app
- **Supports reliable recipe imports** — helps fund the infrastructure behind imports
- **Helps fund ongoing improvements** while keeping the free plan available for everyone

## Pricing

- **\$2.99 USD/month**
- **\$24.99 USD/year**

Both plans include the same features. The yearly plan works out cheaper if you know you'd like to stick with Souschef Plus for a while.

## Free vs. Souschef Plus

|  | Free | Souschef Plus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Recipe imports | 5 per week | Unlimited |
| Ads while browsing and importing | Shown | Removed |

## How billing works

Souschef Plus is billed entirely through your phone's app store — not by Souschef directly:

- **iPhone and iPad** — subscribe and manage your plan through the **App Store** (Apple ID)
- **Android** — subscribe and manage your plan through **Google Play**

Your payment method, billing history, and renewal settings all live inside your Apple or Google account.

## Why a paid subscription?

Souschef is built by a small, independent team. Every Souschef Plus subscription helps keep recipe imports reliable, supports free users, and allows the app to continue improving. If you enjoy using Souschef, subscribing is a direct way to help keep it going.
