You can cancel your Souschef subscription at any time — there are no penalties or fees for cancelling. Because subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play rather than through Souschef directly, you'll handle the cancellation inside your phone's app store settings. It only takes a minute.

## How to cancel

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iPhone (iOS)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open your iPhone Settings">
        Go to the **Settings** app on your iPhone (the grey gear icon — not inside the Souschef app).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap your name">
        At the very top of Settings, tap your name to open your **Apple ID** settings.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap 'Subscriptions'">
        Scroll down and tap **Subscriptions** to see a list of all your active and expired App Store subscriptions.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Souschef">
        Find **Souschef** in the list and tap on it to open the subscription details.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap 'Cancel Subscription'">
        Tap **Cancel Subscription** at the bottom of the page and confirm when prompted. That's it — you're cancelled.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open the Google Play app">
        Open the **Google Play** app on your Android phone.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap your profile icon">
        In the top-right corner, tap your **profile icon** to open your account menu.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Go to Payments & subscriptions">
        Tap **Payments & subscriptions**, then tap **Subscriptions**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Souschef">
        Find **Souschef** in your list of subscriptions and tap on it.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap 'Cancel subscription'">
        Tap **Cancel subscription** and follow the on-screen prompts to confirm. Your subscription will be cancelled.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Cancelling stops future billing, but you'll continue to have full access to your subscription features until the end of your current billing period. You won't be charged again after that.
</Note>
