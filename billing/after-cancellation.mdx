Cancelling Souschef Plus doesn't delete any of your saved recipes — your recipe library stays intact. Here's what to expect once you cancel.

## What happens to your recipes

- **Your recipes stay in your account.** Everything you've imported and saved remains in your library, tied to your Souschef account.
- **You keep Plus features until the end of your billing period.** If you cancel mid-month or mid-year, unlimited imports and ad-free browsing continue until your paid period ends.
- **Your account returns to the free plan.** After your paid period ends, you'll be back on the free plan — up to 5 recipe imports per week, with ads shown while browsing and importing.

## Will I lose my recipes?

No. Cancelling Souschef Plus does not delete any recipes from your library. To permanently remove your recipes and account data, you'd need to [delete your account](/account/delete-account) separately.
