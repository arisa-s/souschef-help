## What you can generally expect

While the specifics depend on how Souschef's subscription is structured when it launches, here's what you should keep in mind:

- **Your recipes are linked to your account.** As long as your Souschef account exists, your saved recipes are associated with it — not just the device you used.
- **Access typically continues until the billing period ends.** If you cancel mid-period, you'll usually keep full access until the subscription naturally expires at the end of that cycle.
- **After expiry, features may change.** Depending on which features are tied to a subscription, some functionality may become unavailable while your recipe data remains in your account.

If you have any concerns about losing access to your data, please reach out to [support@souschefapp.com](mailto:support@souschefapp.com) before cancelling — we're happy to explain exactly what will happen.
