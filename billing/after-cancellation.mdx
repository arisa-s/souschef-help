Cancelling Souschef Plus doesn't delete any of your saved recipes — your recipe library stays intact. Here's what to expect once you cancel.

## What happens to your recipes

- **Your recipes stay in your account.** Everything you've imported and saved remains in your library, tied to your Souschef account. Every recipe you import is yours to keep — recipes don't expire.
- **You keep Plus features until the end of your billing period.** If you cancel mid-month or mid-year, unlimited imports and an ad-free import experience continue until your paid period ends.
- **Your account returns to the free plan.** After your paid period ends, you'll be back on the free plan — up to 3 new recipe imports per week, with ads during recipe imports. You can still view, organize, edit, search, and cook from every recipe already in your library. The weekly limit only affects new imports.

## Will I lose my recipes?

No. Cancelling Souschef Plus does not delete any recipes from your library. Your recipes stay fully accessible on the free plan. To permanently remove your recipes and account data, you'd need to [delete your account](/account/delete-account) separately.
