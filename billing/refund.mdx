Because Souschef subscriptions are processed and billed by Apple or Google — not by Souschef directly — refund requests need to go through those platforms. The good news is that both Apple and Google have straightforward refund request processes you can use online, usually without needing to contact anyone.

## How to request a refund

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iPhone (iOS) — Apple">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Visit reportaproblem.apple.com">
        On any browser, go to [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign in with your Apple ID">
        Sign in using the **Apple ID** that was used to purchase the Souschef subscription.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Find the Souschef charge">
        Browse your purchase history or use the search to find the **Souschef** charge you'd like to dispute.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap 'Request a Refund'">
        Select the charge and tap **Request a Refund**. Choose a reason from the options provided and submit your request. Apple will review it and follow up via email.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android — Google Play">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Visit your Google Play subscriptions page">
        Go to [play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions](https://play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions) and sign in with the Google account used for the purchase.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Find Souschef">
        Locate the **Souschef** subscription in your list.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Request a refund or contact support">
        Depending on your purchase date and Google's refund eligibility, you may see a **Request a refund** option directly. If not, tap **Get help** to contact Google Play support and explain your situation.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Info>
  According to Souschef's [Terms of Service](https://www.trysouschef.com/toc), subscriptions are non-refundable except as expressly provided in those Terms. Apple and Google each have their own independent refund policies, which may be more flexible — so it's always worth submitting a request through the platform where you purchased.
</Info>

## Have a billing issue that isn't covered?

If you believe there's been a billing error or you have a concern that Apple or Google's refund process doesn't address, please reach out to us directly at [support@souschefapp.com](mailto:support@souschefapp.com). We'll do our best to help figure out next steps.
