Souschef currently lets you import and save recipes without a strict cap — all features are free at this time. This page explains the current state of import limits and what to expect if limits are introduced in the future.

## Current import limits

Right now, Souschef is **free to use** with no published limit on the number of recipes you can import and save. You can fill your collection with as many recipes as you like.

<Info>
  All features — including unlimited recipe saving — are free at this time. This may change in the future, and we'll give you clear notice before anything does. See the [Free plan](/billing/free-plan) page for the full list of what's included.
</Info>

## Will limits be introduced in the future?

Souschef may introduce a subscription tier in the future that includes higher or unlimited import limits as a premium feature. If and when that happens, free accounts may have a limit on the number of recipes they can save.

## What happens if you reach a limit?

If a recipe import limit is introduced, you'll see a notification inside the app when you're approaching or have reached it. At that point, you'd have the option to:

- **Upgrade to a subscription** to continue importing recipes without restrictions.
- **Remove older recipes** from your collection to free up space.
