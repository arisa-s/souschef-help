Souschef's free plan includes up to **5 recipe imports per week**. If you'd like to import more, [Souschef Plus](/billing/subscription-benefits) removes the limit for unlimited imports.

## How the free import limit works

- Free users can import up to **5 recipes per week** from any [supported source](/importing/supported-sources) — Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, YouTube, Pinterest, recipe websites, photos, and pasted text.
- The count applies to imports, not to the total number of recipes in your library — recipes you've already saved stay in your collection.

## What happens when you reach your 5 imports

When you've used your 5 weekly imports, you'll see a message in the app the next time you try to import. From there you can:

- **Wait until your weekly count resets** — your next imports will be available then.
- **Upgrade to [Souschef Plus](/billing/subscription-benefits)** for unlimited imports and an ad-free experience.

## Souschef Plus — unlimited imports

Souschef Plus removes the weekly import limit and removes ads from browsing and importing.

- **\$2.99 USD/month**
- **\$24.99 USD/year**

See the [Souschef Plus](/billing/subscription-benefits) page for details, or [subscribe](/billing/subscribe) directly from the App Store or Google Play.
