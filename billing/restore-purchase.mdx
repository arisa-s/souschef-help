If you've reinstalled Souschef or switched to a new phone and your subscription doesn't seem to be active anymore, don't worry — your purchase isn't lost. You can restore it in just a few steps. Since subscriptions are handled by Apple or Google, the restore process happens through your device's app store rather than inside Souschef directly.

## How to restore your subscription

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iPhone (iOS)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Souschef">
        Launch the Souschef app on your iPhone.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Go to Settings or Account">
        Tap the **Settings** or **Account** section within the app. This is usually accessible from your profile or the main menu.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap 'Restore Purchases'">
        Look for a **Restore Purchases** option and tap it. The app will check with Apple for any active subscriptions linked to your Apple ID.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sign in if prompted">
        If asked, sign in with the **Apple ID** you used when you originally subscribed. Your subscription should be restored momentarily.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Sign in to the same Google account">
        Make sure you're signed in to your Android device with the **same Google account** you used when you first subscribed to Souschef.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Reinstall or reopen Souschef">
        If you've just reinstalled the app, open it and wait a moment. Google Play subscriptions are designed to restore automatically when you're signed into the correct account.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check your subscription status">
        If your subscription still isn't showing, open the **Google Play** app, tap your profile icon, and go to **Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions** to confirm your Souschef subscription is still active.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Make sure you're signed in with the same account — Apple ID or Google account — that was used when you originally made the purchase. Subscriptions are tied to that account, not just the device.
</Note>
