Common questions about the free plan, [Souschef Plus](/billing/subscription-benefits), and billing. If your question isn't answered here, [reach out to support](/troubleshooting/contact-support).

## About the free plan and Souschef Plus

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Souschef free?">
    Yes! Souschef is free to download and includes almost all of its features.

    Free users can import up to **3 recipes each week** and may see ads during recipe imports. Every recipe you import is yours to keep — recipes don't expire, and the weekly limit only affects new imports.

    [Souschef Plus](/billing/subscription-benefits) removes the weekly import limit and provides an ad-free import experience. Souschef Plus also helps support ongoing development of the app.

    Most features—including Cooking Mode, Shopping Lists, Pantry, Nutrition, AI Tagging, AI Recipe Translation, AI Discovery, and recipe organization—are available on the free plan.

    See [Free vs Souschef Plus](/billing/free-plan) for a complete comparison.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Souschef Plus?">
    Souschef Plus is the paid subscription that removes the free plan's weekly import limit and removes ads from the recipe import flow.

    Souschef is an independently built app. Souschef Plus helps cover the cost of running recipe imports, keeps the free plan available for everyone, and supports ongoing improvements. See [About Souschef Plus](/billing/subscription-benefits) for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does Souschef Plus include?">
    - Unlimited recipe imports
    - Ad-free import experience
    - Supports reliable recipe imports
    - Helps fund ongoing improvements while keeping the free plan available

    Most product features—Cooking Mode, Shopping Lists, Pantry, Nutrition, AI Tagging, AI Recipe Translation, AI Discovery, and more—are available on the free plan. Plus primarily removes the weekly import limit and ads from the import flow.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How much does Souschef Plus cost?">
    - **\$2.99 USD/month**
    - **\$24.99 USD/year** (includes a **7-day free trial**)

    Both plans include the same features. You can cancel during the free trial and you won't be charged.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens after I use my 3 weekly imports?">
    You don't lose access to anything you've already imported. Every recipe you import is yours to keep — recipes don't expire, and you can continue viewing, organizing, editing, searching, and cooking from your library.

    The weekly limit only affects **new recipe imports**. Once you've used your 3 imports for the week, you can wait until your count resets or upgrade to [Souschef Plus](/billing/subscription-benefits) for unlimited imports and an ad-free import experience.

    See [Recipe import limits](/billing/import-limits) for more detail.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Managing your subscription

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes. You can cancel Souschef Plus at any time through your phone's app store settings — there are no cancellation fees. See [How to cancel](/billing/cancel) for step-by-step instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I cancel?">
    You'll keep Souschef Plus features (unlimited imports and an ad-free import experience) until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account returns to the free plan — 3 new imports per week, with ads during recipe imports.

    Your recipes stay yours to keep. You can continue viewing, organizing, editing, searching, and cooking from everything already in your library. See [what happens after you cancel](/billing/after-cancellation) for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will I lose my recipes?">
    No. Cancelling Souschef Plus does not delete any of your saved recipes. Every recipe you import is yours to keep — your library stays intact and fully accessible on the free plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I restore my purchase?">
    If Souschef Plus isn't active after reinstalling or switching devices, you can restore it through the App Store or Google Play. See [Restore your purchase](/billing/restore-purchase) for the exact steps.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get a refund?">
    Refund requests go through Apple or Google, since that's where Souschef Plus is billed. See [Refunds](/billing/refund) for how to submit a request.

## Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who processes my payment?">
    Apple (App Store) on iPhone and iPad, and Google (Google Play) on Android. Souschef does not process payments directly — your payment method, billing history, and renewal settings live inside your Apple or Google account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
    Yes — the yearly Souschef Plus plan includes a **7-day free trial**. You get full Plus benefits during the trial. If you cancel before the trial ends, you won't be charged. The monthly plan does not include a free trial.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a family plan?">
    No. Souschef Plus is an individual subscription and does not offer a family plan or shared access across Family Sharing / family library accounts. Each person who wants Plus needs their own subscription.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
