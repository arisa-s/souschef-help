The full Souschef experience is available as a free app for **iOS** and **Android**. Shared recipe links also open in a lightweight web viewer, so anyone can read a recipe in their browser without installing the app.

## iOS (iPhone & iPad)

Souschef is available for iPhone and iPad running a recent version of iOS/iPadOS. It was the first platform Souschef launched on, so the iOS version is well-established and packed with features.

[Download on the App Store →](https://apps.apple.com/app/souschef-recipe-organizer/id6468939420)

## Web (shared recipes)

Souschef includes a lightweight web experience designed primarily for recipe sharing.

When someone opens a shared Souschef recipe link in their browser, they can:

- View the recipe
- Read ingredients and instructions
- [Adjust serving sizes](/using/adjust-servings)
- Switch between supported [measurement systems](/using/convert-measurements) (Metric, US Customary, and more)

The web version is intended for viewing and sharing recipes with people who don't have the app installed. It is not a full desktop app.

<Note>
  To import recipes, organize your library, and access the full feature set, use
  the iOS or Android app.
</Note>

### Features that require the mobile app

The full Souschef experience is available only in the iOS and Android apps. Features that currently require the mobile app include:

- Importing recipes
- Organizing your recipe library
- [Cooking Mode](/using/cooking-mode)
- [Shopping Lists](/using/shopping-list)
- [Pantry](/using/pantry)
- [AI Discovery](/using/ai-discovery)
- [AI Translation](/using/ai-translation)
- [AI Tagging](/using/ai-tagging)
- Account management
- Subscription management
