Souschef is available as a free mobile app for both **iOS** and **Android** — so whether you're team iPhone or team Android, you're covered. Below you'll find everything you need to know about downloading the app on your device.

## iOS (iPhone & iPad)

Souschef is available for iPhone and iPad running a recent version of iOS/iPadOS. It was the first platform Souschef launched on, so the iOS version is well-established and packed with features.

[Download on the App Store →](https://apps.apple.com/app/souschef-recipe-organizer/id6468939420)

</Info>

## Android

Android support arrived in **February 2025**, bringing Souschef to Android phones and tablets. If you've been waiting to try it on your Android device, now's the time!

[Get it on Google Play →](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.souschef.app)

<Info>
  📸 Screenshot needed: the Souschef Google Play listing page showing the install button
</Info>

<Note>
  There is no desktop version of Souschef currently available. Souschef is a mobile app designed to be with you in the kitchen — on your phone or tablet.
</Note>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Souschef on a tablet?">

  <Accordion title="Is there a web version of Souschef?">
    Not at this time. Souschef is a mobile-only app, available on iOS and Android. There is no web or desktop version currently available.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Souschef free?">
    Yes! Souschef is free to download and the vast majority of features are free.
