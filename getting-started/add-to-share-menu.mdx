---

The fastest way to save a recipe is never having to leave the app you're already in. When you pin Souschef to your phone's share menu, a single tap on the Share button in Instagram, Safari, Chrome, TikTok, or any other app is all it takes to send a recipe straight to your collection. It takes less than a minute to set up, and you'll wonder how you ever lived without it.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iPhone (iOS)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open any browser app and tap the Share button">
        Open Safari (or any browser) and navigate to any webpage. Tap the **Share** button — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing upward, and you'll usually find it at the bottom of the screen.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Scroll right and tap More">
        In the row of app icons that appears in the share sheet, swipe right until you see the **More** button (it looks like three dots). Tap it.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Edit">
        In the top-right corner of the screen that appears, tap **Edit**. This lets you customise which apps appear in your share menu.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Find Souschef and tap the plus button">
        Scroll down through the list until you find **Souschef**. Tap the green **+** button to its left to add it to your favourites.

        <Note>
          If Souschef doesn't appear in the list, try restarting your phone. A restart typically refreshes your share options and will make Souschef show up.
        </Note>
      </Step>
      <Step title="Drag Souschef to the top of the list">
        Once it's in your favourites, press and hold the three-line handle to the right of Souschef and drag it to the top of the list. Tap **Done** to save your changes.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open any browser app and tap the Share button">
        Open Chrome (or any browser) and navigate to any webpage. Tap the **Share** button — you'll usually find it in the browser's menu or toolbar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Scroll to the bottom of the share sheet">
        The share sheet will pop up showing a row of apps. Scroll down past the top row to see the full list of apps that can receive shared content. Look for **Souschef** in the list.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Long-press on Souschef">
        Press and hold your finger on the **Souschef** icon for a moment until a small menu appears.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Pin Souschef">
        Tap **Pin Souschef** from the menu. Souschef will now appear at the top of your share sheet every time you share something.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Once pinned, you'll see Souschef's icon whenever you tap Share in any app — including Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, and recipe websites. Just tap it to send the recipe straight to your collection.
</Note>
