Whether you're saving a recipe spotted on Instagram, a YouTube cooking video that caught your eye, or a family favourite clipped from a food blog, Souschef is the one place where all your recipes live together. It's a free mobile app designed for everyday home cooks who are tired of juggling browser bookmarks, screenshots, and scribbled notes. With Souschef, you can import any recipe in a few taps, then cook from it with confidence — smart timers, adjustable servings, and a built-in shopping list included.

## Where can I import recipes from?

Souschef can pull recipes from a wide range of places, including:

- **Recipe websites and food blogs** — almost any site with a structured recipe
- **Instagram** — posts and reels
- **TikTok** — recipe videos
- **YouTube** — cooking videos
- **Pinterest** — recipe pins
- **Photos and screenshots** — snap or upload an image and Souschef will scan it for you

We're always working to expand support for more sources. If there's a platform you'd love to see added, drop us a note at [team@nekonote.co](mailto:team@nekonote.co).

## Is Souschef free?

Yes! Souschef is free to download from both the Apple App Store and Google Play. All features are free at this time (subject to change in the future). There are no hidden fees or premium tiers to worry about right now — just download and start cooking.

## Where is Souschef available?

Souschef is available on both **iOS** and **Android**:

- 🍎 **iPhone & iPad** — [Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/souschef-recipe-organizer/id6468939420)
- 🤖 **Android phones & tablets** — [Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.souschef.app)

There is no desktop or web version at this time. Souschef is a mobile-first experience built for cooking in the kitchen, on the go, and everywhere in between.
