Too many recipes saved in too many places? Between Instagram posts, TikTok videos, Facebook shares, YouTube, browser bookmarks, screenshots, and handwritten notes, it's easy for a great recipe to get lost. Souschef brings them all together in one free mobile app. Import recipes in a few taps, keep them beautifully organized, and enjoy a smoother cooking experience with helpful AI and smart cooking tools.

## Where can I import recipes from?

Souschef can pull recipes from a wide range of places, including:

- **Instagram** — [posts and reels](/importing/import-from-instagram)
- **TikTok** — [recipe videos](/importing/import-from-tiktok)
- **Facebook** — [posts, videos, and reels](/importing/import-from-facebook)
- **YouTube** — [cooking videos](/importing/import-from-youtube)
- **Pinterest** — [recipe pins](/importing/import-from-pinterest)
- **Recipe websites and food blogs** — [almost any site](/importing/import-from-website)
- **Photos and screenshots** — [scan any image](/importing/import-from-photos)
- **Plain text** — [paste text or write manually](/importing/import-from-plain-text)

We're always working to expand support for more sources. If there's a platform you'd love to see added, drop us a note at [team@nekonote.co](mailto:team@nekonote.co).

## Is Souschef free?

Yes — Souschef is free to download from the Apple App Store and Google Play. The free plan includes up to **5 recipe imports per week**, with ads shown while browsing and importing.

**Souschef Plus** adds unlimited recipe imports and removes ads for **\$2.99 USD/month** or **\$24.99 USD/year**. Every subscription helps keep recipe imports reliable, supports free users, and allows the app to keep improving. See [About Souschef Plus](/billing/subscription-benefits) for details.

## Where is Souschef available?

Souschef is available on both **iOS** and **Android**:

- 🍎 **iPhone & iPad** — [Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/souschef-recipe-organizer/id6468939420)
- 🤖 **Android phones & tablets** — [Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.souschef.app)

There is no desktop or web version at this time. Souschef is a mobile-first experience built for cooking in the kitchen, on the go, and everywhere in between.
