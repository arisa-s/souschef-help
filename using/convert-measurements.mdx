Not everyone cooks in the same units — and that's perfectly fine! Souschef lets you convert ingredient measurements on the fly so you can cook in whatever units feel most natural to you. Found a great recipe from a UK food blog that uses grams? No problem. Following an American recipe but you only own a kitchen scale? Souschef has you covered.

## How to convert measurements

There are a couple of ways to access measurement conversions:

- **Tap an ingredient quantity** — Tap directly on an ingredient's amount to see conversion options for that ingredient.
- **Use the measurement toggle** — Look for the measurement/unit toggle in the recipe view to switch the whole recipe between unit systems at once. *(TODO: confirm the exact location of this toggle in the current app UI)*

<Info>Conversions are calculated based on standard cooking measurements. For the most accurate results with dry ingredients like flour or cocoa powder, a kitchen scale is always your best bet — volume measurements can vary depending on how tightly packed the ingredient is.</Info>

<Tip>This feature is especially handy when following a recipe from another country — just switch to your preferred units and cook with confidence, no mental arithmetic required.</Tip>
