Receipt scanning is the fastest way to update your [Pantry](/using/pantry) after a grocery run. Snap a photo of your receipt, and Souschef reads the line items, matches them to Pantry categories, and adds them to the right location — fridge, freezer, or pantry — automatically.

## How to scan a receipt

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Pantry and tap 'Scan receipt'">
    Tap the **Pantry** tab, then tap the **Scan receipt** option (usually behind the **\+** button).

    <Info>
      📸 Screenshot needed: the Pantry add options with 'Scan receipt' highlighted.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take a photo of the full receipt">
    Lay the receipt flat and take a clear, well-lit photo. If the receipt is long, you can capture it in multiple photos.

    <Info>
      📸 Screenshot needed: the camera view aimed at a grocery receipt with edge-detection guidance.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the detected items">
    Souschef reads the receipt and shows you a list of detected items with a suggested location and category for each one. Untick anything you don't want to add (non-food items, for example), or edit an item's location before saving.

    <Info>
      📸 Screenshot needed: the detected-items review screen with checkboxes and per-item location dropdowns.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Pantry">
    Tap **Add to Pantry**. Your inventory is updated in seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  For best results, take the photo in good light on a flat surface. Curled or crumpled receipts can confuse the scanner — flatten them out with your palm before shooting.
</Tip>

## What Souschef does with your receipt

Souschef reads the receipt only to extract line items. The receipt image and its contents aren't shared publicly — it's used to build your Pantry entries.

## After you scan

Once your Pantry is up to date, every future [shopping list](/using/shopping-list) you generate from a recipe will automatically skip ingredients you already have. Recipe ingredients that match your Pantry will show an **In Pantry** badge, so you always know what you're covered for.
