All your imported recipes are stored right inside Souschef and available offline — no internet connection needed once a recipe is saved. Whether you've imported a handful of family favorites or built up a library of hundreds of dishes, Souschef's search makes finding exactly what you're looking for a breeze.

## Searching for a recipe

Finding a recipe takes just a few taps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the search bar">
    Open Souschef and tap the **search bar** at the top of your recipe list.

    <Info>
      📸 Screenshot needed: Home screen with the search bar highlighted at the top of the recipe list.
    </Info>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Type a keyword">
    Type a dish name, a main ingredient, or anything you remember about the recipe.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your recipe">
    Results appear as you type, so you'll usually spot what you're looking for before you even finish typing.

    <Info>
      📸 Screenshot needed: Search results updating in real time as the user types a keyword.
    </Info>

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Give your recipes descriptive names when saving so they're easy to find later.
  For example, "Mom's Chicken Tikka Masala" is much easier to search for than
  just "Chicken."
</Tip>

## Browsing your recipe collection

All of your saved recipes are displayed in the main view when you open the app. Scroll through your collection to browse what you've got on hand.
