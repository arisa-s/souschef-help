All your imported recipes are stored right inside Souschef and available offline — no internet connection needed once a recipe is saved. Whether you've imported a handful of family favorites or built up a library of hundreds of dishes, Souschef's search makes finding exactly what you're looking for a breeze.

## Searching for a recipe

Finding a recipe takes just a few taps:

1. Open Souschef and tap the **search bar** at the top of your recipe list.
2. Type a keyword — the dish name, a main ingredient, or anything you remember about the recipe.
3. Results appear as you type, so you'll usually spot what you're looking for before you even finish typing.

<Tip>Give your recipes descriptive names when saving so they're easy to find later. For example, "Mom's Chicken Tikka Masala" is much easier to search for than just "Chicken."</Tip>

## Browsing your recipe collection

All of your saved recipes are displayed in the main view when you open the app. Scroll through your collection to browse what you've got on hand.
