Quick answers to the questions we get most about [Pantry](/using/pantry). Can't find yours here? [Contact support](/troubleshooting/contact-support) and we'll help.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I have to add expiration dates?">
    No. Expiration dates are completely optional. Add them for perishables like dairy or produce if it's helpful, and skip them for shelf-stable items like rice or salt.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does Souschef know an ingredient is 'in my Pantry'?">
    When you generate a [shopping list](/using/shopping-list) from a recipe, Souschef matches each recipe ingredient against your Pantry by name. If a match is found, that ingredient is skipped on the shopping list and shown with an **In Pantry** badge on the recipe. If Souschef isn't sure, it errs on the side of adding it to your list so you don't run out unexpectedly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does Pantry track how much of each ingredient I have?">
    You can set an optional quantity on each item, but Souschef doesn't automatically decrement quantities when you cook a recipe. Update quantities manually as you use things up, or scan your next grocery receipt to refresh your inventory.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What locations can I use?">
    Every Pantry item lives in one of three locations: **Fridge**, **Freezer**, or **Pantry**. Within each location, items are grouped into categories like produce, dairy, meat, grains, and spices.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I add an ingredient to my shopping list even if it's in my Pantry?">
    Yes. When you generate a shopping list, the ingredient is skipped automatically — but you can override that and add it manually if you're running low. Or, tap the ingredient inside the recipe and add it to your shopping list directly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if the receipt scanner reads an item wrong?">
    You can edit or untick any item on the review screen before adding it to your Pantry. If an item is being misread consistently, please [contact support](/troubleshooting/contact-support) with the receipt image — it helps us improve the scanner.
  </Accordion>
