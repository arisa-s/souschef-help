Cooking Mode is the heart of Souschef — it transforms any recipe into a hands-free cooking assistant right on your phone. It's designed to tackle all those little frustrations that come with cooking from a mobile device: a screen that dims at the worst moment, juggling a separate timer app, losing track of ingredient quantities mid-step. Cooking Mode handles all of it so you can focus on what actually matters — the food.

## What Cooking Mode does

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Step-by-step navigation" icon="arrow-right">
    Move through your recipe one step at a time so you always know exactly where you are and what comes next.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Keeps your screen awake" icon="sun">
    Your phone stays lit the entire time you're in Cooking Mode — no more tapping the screen with floury hands.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Smart timers" icon="clock">
    Time mentions in recipe steps are automatically detected and turned into one-tap countdown timers.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Ingredient referencing" icon="list">
    Tap any ingredient name in a step to instantly see its quantity — no scrolling back to the top of the recipe.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Prep checklist" icon="check">
    All prep tasks (like chopping, mincing, or preheating) are listed upfront as a checklist before cooking begins.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Multiple recipe windows" icon="table-columns">
    Open a second recipe in a new window while keeping your first recipe — and all its timers — running in the background.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Serving adjustments" icon="sliders">
    Change the number of servings right inside Cooking Mode and all ingredient quantities recalculate automatically.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## During cooking

Once you're in Cooking Mode, here are a few handy things you can do:

- **Check an ingredient quantity** — Tap any ingredient name mentioned in a step to see how much you need. Quantities automatically reflect your chosen serving size.
- **Start a timer** — When a step mentions a time (like "simmer for 10 minutes"), tap it to kick off a countdown right in the app. No need to switch to a separate timer app.
- **Open a second recipe** — Tap the multi-window button to open another recipe alongside your current one. Your timers keep running in the background the whole time.

<Tip>
  Your phone screen stays awake the entire time you're in Cooking Mode — no more tapping to wake it up mid-recipe!
</Tip>
