Cooking Mode is the heart of Souschef — it turns any recipe into a step-by-step cooking guide on your phone. It isn't fully hands-free yet, but it cuts down how often you need to touch the screen. It tackles common mobile cooking frustrations: a screen that dims at the worst moment, juggling a separate timer app, losing track of ingredient quantities mid-step. Cooking Mode handles all of that so you can focus on the food.

## What Cooking Mode does

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Step-by-step navigation" icon="arrow-right">
    Move through your recipe one step at a time so you always know exactly where you are and what comes next.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Keeps your screen awake" icon="sun">
    Your phone stays lit the entire time you're in Cooking Mode — so you tap less just to keep the screen on.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Smart timers" icon="clock">
    Time mentions in recipe steps are automatically detected and turned into one-tap countdown timers.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Ingredient referencing" icon="list">
    Tap any ingredient name in a step to instantly see its quantity — no scrolling back to the top of the recipe.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Prep checklist" icon="check">
    All prep tasks (like chopping, mincing, or preheating) are listed upfront as a checklist before cooking begins.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Multiple recipe windows" icon="table-columns">
    Open a second recipe in a new window while keeping your first recipe — and all its timers — running in the background.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Serving adjustments" icon="sliders">
    Change the number of servings right inside Cooking Mode and all ingredient quantities recalculate automatically.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to start Cooking Mode

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a recipe">
    Tap any recipe in your Souschef library to open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Step by Step">
    Tap **Step by Step** to launch Cooking Mode.

    <img
      src="/images/screenshots/cooking-mode/step-by-step-button.webp"
      alt="Recipe detail with the Step by Step button highlighted"
      className="help-screenshot"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the prep checklist">
    Before the first cooking step, you'll see a checklist of all the prep tasks for this recipe — including things like preheating the oven. Work through the list and check each item off as you go.

    <img
      src="/images/screenshots/cooking-mode/prep-and-preheat.webp"
      alt="Cooking Mode showing the Prep Ingredients and Preheat Oven checklist with tasks like thinly sliced onions and grated parmesan"
      className="help-screenshot"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the steps">
    Tap **Next** to move through each cooking step at your own pace. Souschef guides you from start to finish. Tap a highlighted time mention to start a [timer](/using/timers) without leaving the recipe.

    <img
      src="/images/screenshots/cooking-mode/cooking-step.webp"
      alt="Cooking Mode step with bake for 1 hour highlighted as a tappable timer"
      className="help-screenshot"
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## During cooking

Once you're in Cooking Mode, here are a few handy things you can do:

### Adjust servings

Tap the servings control at the top to scale the recipe while you cook. Ingredient quantities update automatically.

<img
  src="/images/screenshots/cooking-mode/servings-button.webp"
  alt="Cooking Mode with the servings button highlighted"
  className="help-screenshot"
/>

### Convert measurements

Tap the convert units button (beaker icon) to switch between measurement systems without leaving Cooking Mode.

<img
  src="/images/screenshots/cooking-mode/convert-units-button.webp"
  alt="Cooking Mode with the convert units button highlighted"
  className="help-screenshot"
/>

### Check an ingredient quantity

Tap any highlighted ingredient name in a step to see how much you need. From there you can also search for more info, find a substitution, or locate it in a supermarket.

<img
  src="/images/screenshots/cooking-mode/ingredient-detail.webp"
  alt="Ingredient detail sheet for onions with options to search on Google, search substitution, or locate it in supermarket"
  className="help-screenshot"
/>

### View the full ingredients list

Tap the list icon to open the full ingredients checklist with quantities.

<img
  src="/images/screenshots/cooking-mode/ingredients-list-button.webp"
  alt="Cooking Mode with the ingredients list button highlighted in the bottom bar"
  className="help-screenshot"
/>

### Start a timer

When a step mentions a time (like "simmer for 10 minutes"), tap it to kick off a countdown right in the app. You can also add a timer manually with the timer button. See [How recipe timers work](/using/timers) for details.

### Open a second recipe

Tap the multi-window button to open another recipe alongside your current one. Your timers keep running in the background the whole time.

<img
  src="/images/screenshots/cooking-mode/open-other-recipe-button.webp"
  alt="Cooking Mode with the open other recipe button highlighted"
  className="help-screenshot"
/>

From there, tap **+ Add Recipe Tab** to open another recipe, or **Finish Cooking** when you're done.

<img
  src="/images/screenshots/cooking-mode/multi-recipe-view.webp"
  alt="Cooking Mode multi-recipe view with Finish Cooking and Add Recipe Tab buttons"
  className="help-screenshot"
/>

<Tip>
  Your phone screen stays awake the entire time you're in Cooking Mode, so you don't have to keep waking it up mid-recipe.
</Tip>
