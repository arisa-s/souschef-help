Souschef's Pantry keeps track of what you already have in your kitchen — in the fridge, freezer, or pantry — so you stop buying duplicates and stop guessing what you can cook tonight. Add items by typing them in, scanning a grocery receipt, or snapping a photo of what's on your shelf, then let Souschef quietly check every recipe you cook against what you already own.

## Pantry locations

Every Pantry item lives in one of three locations, so you can tell at a glance where an ingredient is stored:

- **Fridge** — chilled ingredients like dairy, produce, opened jars.
- **Freezer** — frozen produce, meat, batches you meal-prepped.
- **Pantry** — shelf-stable items like grains, cans, spices, oils.

## Categories and expiration dates

Within each location, items are grouped into **categories** — produce, dairy, meat, grains, spices, and more — so a stocked kitchen never looks overwhelming.

Each item can also have an optional **expiration date**. Expiration dates are entirely optional, but adding them means Souschef can surface what's about to expire so nothing goes to waste.

## Add items to your Pantry

There are three ways to add items — pick whichever fits the moment.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Type it in">
    Tap the **\+** button in Pantry, type the item name, choose a location and category, and (optionally) add a quantity and expiration date. Great for one-off additions or when you're mid-cooking.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Scan a receipt">
    Just got home from the grocery store? Snap a photo of your receipt and Souschef will read the items and add them to your Pantry automatically. See [Receipt Scanning](/using/receipt-scanning) for details.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Pantry photo recognition">
    Open your fridge, snap a photo, and Souschef uses AI to detect the ingredients it sees and offer them for you to add. Perfect for a first-time Pantry setup.

## Pantry \+ Shopping List integration

This is where Pantry starts saving you real time and money. When you [generate a shopping list](/using/shopping-list) from a recipe:

- Ingredients you **already have** in your Pantry are **not** automatically added to the shopping list.
- Any matching ingredient still shown in the recipe is marked with an **In Pantry** badge, so you can see at a glance what you're covered for.
- If you'd rather add something anyway (say, you're running low), you can override the skip and add it manually.

## After shopping: keep your Pantry up to date

When you get home from the store, open Souschef and [scan your receipt](/using/receipt-scanning). Souschef reads the line items, matches them to your Pantry, and updates your inventory in seconds — no manual entry required.

<Tip>
  Even if you only stock the essentials in your Pantry — oil, salt, common spices, staples like rice and pasta — you'll immediately notice a shorter, smarter shopping list. Start small and add items over time.
</Tip>
