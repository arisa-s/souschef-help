Whether you're cooking a cozy dinner for one or feeding a hungry crowd, Souschef takes the math out of scaling a recipe. Change the number of servings and every ingredient quantity updates instantly — no calculator required.

## How to adjust servings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a recipe">
    Tap any recipe in your Souschef library to open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the servings control">
    Look for the serving size near the recipe details — tap the serving number or use the **+** and **–** buttons to increase or decrease the count.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch the quantities update">
    All ingredient quantities recalculate automatically as soon as you change the serving size. No manual math needed!
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can also adjust servings in two other places:

- **Inside Cooking Mode** — change servings on the fly while you're actively cooking, and all ingredient references update in real time.
- **When adding to your shopping list** — adjust servings before adding ingredients to make sure you buy exactly the right amounts at the store.

<Tip>Adjust servings before adding a recipe to your shopping list to get the right quantities for grocery shopping — that way your list is accurate from the start.</Tip>
