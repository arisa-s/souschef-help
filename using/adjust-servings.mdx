Good news — adjusting servings is available to everyone using Souschef.

Whether you're cooking a cozy dinner for one or feeding a hungry crowd, Souschef takes the math out of scaling a recipe. Change the number of servings and every ingredient quantity updates instantly — no calculator required. When you share a recipe link, people viewing it in a browser can adjust servings too.

## How to adjust servings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a recipe">
    Tap any recipe in your Souschef library to open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the servings control">
    On the **Ingredients** tab, tap the servings button (for example, **9 cookies**).

    <img
      src="/images/screenshots/adjust-servings/servings-button.webp"
      alt="Recipe detail with the servings button highlighted on the Ingredients tab"
      className="help-screenshot"
    />

  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust and apply">
    Use the up and down arrows — or type a number — to set the new serving size, then tap **Apply**. All ingredient quantities recalculate automatically.

    <img
      src="/images/screenshots/adjust-servings/adjust-servings-sheet.webp"
      alt="Adjust Serving sheet with up and down arrows, Cancel, and Apply"
      className="help-screenshot"
    />

  </Step>
</Steps>

You can also adjust servings in two other places:

- **Inside Cooking Mode** — change servings on the fly while you're actively cooking, and all ingredient references update in real time.
- **When adding to your shopping list** — adjust servings before adding ingredients to make sure you buy exactly the right amounts at the store.

<Tip>
  Adjust servings before adding a recipe to your shopping list to get the right quantities for grocery shopping — that way your list is accurate from the start.
</Tip>
