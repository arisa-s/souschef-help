Souschef's Smart Shopping List turns your recipes into an organized grocery list in seconds. Better yet, it automatically sorts everything by store aisle — dairy with dairy, produce with produce — so you can move through the store in one smooth loop without doubling back. Say goodbye to the scattered, hard-to-read notes you used to bring to the grocery store!

## Create a shopping list from a recipe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Import a recipe">
    Make sure the recipe you want to shop for is already saved in Souschef. If you haven't imported it yet, check out [Import your first recipe](/getting-started/import-your-first-recipe) to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Add to Shopping List">
    Open the recipe and tap the **Add to Shopping List** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust the serving size">
    Choose how many servings you're making — the ingredient quantities will update automatically to match.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check off what you already have">
    Raid your fridge and pantry! Check off any ingredients you already have on hand so they won't be added to your list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Add items">
    Hit the **Add items** button and your shopping list is ready — automatically sorted by store aisle so you can shop efficiently.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>Check off what you already have in your fridge and pantry before adding items — it's the easiest way to avoid buying duplicates of things you already own.</Tip>

## Add multiple recipes at once

Planning meals for the whole week? No problem. Just repeat the steps above for each recipe you want to cook. Souschef will **merge all the ingredients together** — so if two recipes both need garlic, it combines them into a single line item. One tidy list, multiple meals.

## Add non-recipe items

Your shopping trip isn't just about dinner — you might need paper towels, dish soap, or a snack for the kids too. Tap the **add** button on your shopping list to type in any item that isn't part of a recipe. Everything stays in one place.

## Share your list

Sending someone else to the store? Or want to coordinate with a partner?

1. Tap **Share** on your shopping list.
2. Send it via text message or any messaging app on your phone.

Your list goes out exactly as it's organized — sorted by aisle and ready to use.
