Souschef's Smart Shopping List turns your recipes into an organized grocery list in seconds. Better yet, it automatically sorts everything by store aisle — dairy with dairy, produce with produce — so you can move through the store in one smooth loop without doubling back. And if you use [Pantry](/using/pantry), any ingredient you already have is skipped automatically, so your list only shows what you actually need to buy. Say goodbye to the scattered, hard-to-read notes you used to bring to the grocery store!

## Create a shopping list from a recipe

<Steps>
  <Step title="Import a recipe">
    Make sure the recipe you want to shop for is already saved in Souschef. If you haven't imported it yet, check out [Import your first recipe](/getting-started/import-your-first-recipe) to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Add to Shopping List">
    Open the recipe and tap the **Add to Shopping List** button.

    <Info>
      📸 Screenshot needed: Recipe detail view with the Add to Shopping List button highlighted.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust the serving size">
    Choose how many servings you're making — the ingredient quantities will update automatically to match.

    <Info>
      📸 Screenshot needed: Serving size adjustment screen shown before adding items to the shopping list.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the 'In Pantry' badges">
    If you use [Pantry](/using/pantry), Souschef automatically checks the recipe against what you already own. Ingredients already in your Pantry are marked with an **In Pantry** badge and are **not** added to the shopping list. You can still add them manually if you're running low.

    You can also uncheck any other ingredient you already have on hand — anything unchecked is skipped from the list.

    <Info>
      📸 Screenshot needed: Ingredient checklist showing an ingredient with the 'In Pantry' badge next to it, and other items unchecked.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Add items">
    Hit the **Add items** button and your shopping list is ready — automatically sorted by store aisle so you can shop efficiently.

    <Info>
      📸 Screenshot needed: Completed shopping list sorted by store aisle (e.g., Produce, Dairy, Spices).
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Keeping your [Pantry](/using/pantry) up to date is the easiest way to avoid buying duplicates. Scan your grocery receipt after each shopping trip and Souschef will automatically skip those ingredients on future shopping lists.
</Tip>

## After shopping: scan your receipt

Just got home from the store? Scan your grocery receipt to update your [Pantry](/using/pantry) inventory automatically — no manual entry needed. See [Receipt Scanning](/using/receipt-scanning).

## Add multiple recipes at once

Planning meals for the whole week? No problem. Just repeat the steps above for each recipe you want to cook. Souschef will **merge all the ingredients together** — so if two recipes both need garlic, it combines them into a single line item. One tidy list, multiple meals.

## Add non-recipe items

Your shopping trip isn't just about dinner — you might need paper towels, dish soap, or a snack for the kids too. Tap the **add** button on your shopping list to type in any item that isn't part of a recipe. Everything stays in one place.

## Share your list

Sending someone else to the store? Or want to coordinate with a partner?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Share">
    Tap **Share** on your shopping list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send it">
    Send it via text message or any messaging app on your phone.

    <Info>
      📸 Screenshot needed: Share sheet appearing from the shopping list with messaging apps visible.
    </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your list goes out exactly as it's organized — sorted by aisle and ready to use.
