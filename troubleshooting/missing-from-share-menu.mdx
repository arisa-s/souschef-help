If you tap **Share** in your browser or another app and Souschef isn't showing up, don't worry — it just needs to be added to your share menu. It's a quick, one-time setup, and once it's done you'll be able to send recipes to Souschef with a single tap. Follow the steps for your device below.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iPhone (iOS)">
    1. Open any browser app (like Safari or Chrome) and tap the **Share** button (the box with an arrow pointing up).
    2. Scroll the row of icons to the right and tap **More**.
    3. Tap **Edit** in the top-right corner.
    4. Scroll through the list to find **Souschef** and tap the **+** button next to it to add it.
    5. Once added, press and drag Souschef to the top of the list so it's easy to find.
    6. Tap **Done** to save your changes.

    Souschef will now appear near the top of your share sheet whenever you tap Share in any app.

    <Note>If Souschef isn't listed at all in the Edit screen, try restarting your phone first — this refreshes your share options list and should make Souschef appear.</Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android">
    1. Open any browser app and tap the **Share** button.
    2. Scroll to the **bottom** of the share options list.
    3. Find **Souschef** and **long-press** on it.
    4. Tap **Pin Souschef**.

    Souschef will now be pinned near the top of your share options for easy access!
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  If Souschef still doesn't appear after following these steps, make sure the Souschef app is installed and fully up to date, then try restarting your phone. This usually does the trick!
</Note>
