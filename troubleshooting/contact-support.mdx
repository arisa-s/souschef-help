## How to reach us

We currently offer support by email. Here's where to send your message depending on what you need:

| Topic                             | Email                                             |
| --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| General support and app questions | [support@nekonote.co](mailto:support@nekonote.co) |
| Privacy and data requests         | [support@nekonote.co](mailto:support@nekonote.co) |
| Feature ideas and feedback        | [team@nekonote.co](mailto:team@nekonote.co)       |

<Tip>
  When reporting a bug, it really helps us if you include: your **device model**
  (e.g., iPhone 15, Pixel 8), your **operating system version** (e.g., iOS 17.4,
  Android 14), and a **brief description of what happened** — and a screenshot
  if you have one. The more detail you share, the faster we can track it down
  and fix it!
</Tip>

<Info>
  We aim to respond within 1–2 business days. Thanks so much for your patience —
  we appreciate it!
</Info>
