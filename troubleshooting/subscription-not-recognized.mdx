If you've subscribed to Souschef but the app doesn't seem to know about it — maybe you're still seeing a paywall, or premium features aren't unlocked — you can usually fix this in just a moment by restoring your purchase. Here's how.

## Restore your subscription

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="iPhone (iOS)">
    1. Open the **Souschef** app.
    2. Go to **Account** or **Settings**.
    3. Tap **Restore Purchases**.
    4. If prompted, sign in with your Apple ID.

    Souschef will check your purchase history with the App Store and re-activate your subscription.

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Android">
    On Android, your subscription should restore automatically as long as you're signed in to the same Google account you used when you subscribed.

    1. Make sure you're signed in to Souschef with your account.
    2. Verify that you're using the same Google account tied to your Google Play purchase.
    3. Close and reopen the app to prompt a refresh.

    If it still isn't recognized after reopening, try the steps in the "Still not working?" section below.

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account you used
  when you originally subscribed. Using a different account is the most common
  reason a subscription doesn't restore.
</Note>

## Still not working?

If restoring your purchase didn't do the trick, here are a few more things to try:

1. **Check that your subscription is still active** — open the App Store (Settings → your name → Subscriptions on iOS) or Google Play (Menu → Subscriptions on Android) and confirm the subscription is active and hasn't expired.
2. **Force-quit and reopen the app** — this prompts Souschef to re-check your subscription status.
3. **Contact support** — email us at [support@nekonote.co](mailto:support@nekonote.co) and include your receipt or purchase confirmation. We'll look into it and get you sorted out.

For a more detailed walkthrough, see the [Restore your purchase](/billing/restore-purchase) guide.
