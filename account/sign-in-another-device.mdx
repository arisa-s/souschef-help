---

Your Souschef recipes are linked to your account, not just your device — so as soon as you sign in on a new phone or tablet, everything you've saved will be right there waiting for you. Just follow the steps below and you'll be back in the kitchen in no time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Souschef on your new device">
    Head to the **App Store** (iOS) or **Google Play** (Android) and download Souschef on your new device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Launch Souschef once it's finished installing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Sign In">
    On the welcome screen, tap **Sign In** — not "Get Started" or "Create Account."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your account details">
    Enter the same email address and password (or use the same sign-in method — for example, Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In) that you used when you first set up Souschef.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your recipes will sync automatically">
    That's it! Your saved recipes will sync to your new device automatically once you're signed in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Make sure to tap **Sign In** rather than creating a new account — if you register a fresh account, your existing recipes won't appear.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Can't remember which email you used to sign up? Check your inboxes for a welcome or confirmation email from Souschef — that'll tell you the address on file.
</Tip>

If you remember your email but have forgotten your password, you can [reset your password](/account/reset-password) quickly from the sign-in screen.

## Still need help?

If you're still having trouble signing in on your new device, our support team is happy to help. Reach out at [support@souschefapp.com](mailto:support@souschefapp.com) or visit our [contact support page](/troubleshooting/contact-support).
