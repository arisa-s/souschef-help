Souschef stores your data securely in the cloud, so your recipes are always available whenever you sign in — whether that's on your phone, a new tablet, or any other device. Here's a plain-language overview of what we store, where it lives, and how it's protected.

## What data is stored

When you use Souschef, we store:

- **Your account information** — your first and last name, and your email address.
- **Your saved recipes** — everything you've added or created inside the app.

That's it. We only collect what's needed to make the app work well for you.

## Where data is stored

Your data is stored using **Supabase**, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider. Here's what that means for your security:

- **Encrypted at rest** — your data is encrypted while it's stored, so it's protected even at the infrastructure level.
- **SOC2 Type 2 compliant** — Supabase meets rigorous, independently audited security standards.
- **Private server infrastructure** — data is held on isolated private servers, typically AWS EC2 instances.
- **Server locations** — servers are located in the **United States** or potentially other countries.

## Data sharing

Souschef **does not sell your personal data** — ever. We only share data in limited circumstances: as needed to operate the app (for example, sending emails you've opted into), to comply with legal obligations, or to protect our rights. We do not share your information with advertisers or data brokers.

## Your rights

You're in control of your data. At any time, you can:

- **Access or update** your personal information directly within the Souschef app.
- **Delete** your account and all associated data from within the app or by contacting [support@nekonote.co](mailto:support@nekonote.co).

Souschef complies with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**, so if you're based in the EU or UK, your data rights are fully respected.

<Note>Souschef does not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies.</Note>

## Still need help?

Have a question about your data that isn't answered here? Reach out to our team at [support@nekonote.co](mailto:support@nekonote.co) or visit our [contact support page](/troubleshooting/contact-support).
