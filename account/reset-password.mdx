Forgotten passwords happen to the best of us! If you can't remember your Souschef password, you can reset it in just a few steps using your email address. No need to contact anyone — the whole process happens right from the app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Souschef and tap Sign In">
    Launch the app and tap **Sign In** on the welcome screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Forgot password?">
    Below the password field, tap the **Forgot password?** link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Type in the email address associated with your Souschef account and tap **Send Reset Link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox">
    Open your email app and look for a password reset message from Souschef. It should arrive within a few minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a new password">
    Tap the link in the email, then choose and confirm a new password. Once saved, you can sign straight back in to Souschef.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If the reset email doesn't appear within a few minutes, check your **spam** or **junk** folder — it can sometimes end up there.
</Tip>

<Note>

</Note>

If you've tried the steps above and are still locked out, drop us a line at [support@nekonote.co](mailto:support@nekonote.co) — we'll get you sorted.
