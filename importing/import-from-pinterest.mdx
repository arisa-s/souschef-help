Importing from Pinterest is included in the free version of Souschef.

Pinterest is a wonderful place to discover recipe ideas, but keeping track of all those pins can quickly turn into a mess. With Souschef, you can import any Pinterest recipe directly into your collection — where you can scale servings, build a shopping list, and cook without the clutter. There are three ways to do it, so pick whichever works best for you!

## How to import a recipe from Pinterest

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Method 1: Share from Pinterest">
    Already looking at a recipe pin in the Pinterest app? Share it straight to Souschef.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Find a recipe pin and tap Share">
        Open the pin you want to save, then tap the **Share** button (the arrow icon) below the image.

        <img
          src="/images/screenshots/pinterest/share-button.webp"
          alt="Pinterest pin with the Share button highlighted"
          className="help-screenshot"
        />
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap More apps">
        In the **Share Pin link** menu, tap **More apps** to open your phone's share sheet.

        <img
          src="/images/screenshots/pinterest/share-more-apps.webp"
          alt="Pinterest Share Pin link menu with More apps highlighted"
          className="help-screenshot"
        />
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap the Souschef icon">
        Tap the **Souschef** icon in the share sheet. Souschef will open and start extracting the recipe automatically.

        <img
          src="/images/screenshots/pinterest/share-sheet.webp"
          alt="Share sheet with the Souschef app icon"
          className="help-screenshot"
        />

        Don't see Souschef in the share sheet? Follow [Add Souschef to your share menu](/getting-started/add-to-share-menu).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Wait while Souschef extracts the recipe">
        Souschef will extract the recipe from the pin. This usually takes up to 30 seconds, and then your recipe will be ready to view, edit, and cook!
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Method 2: In-app Explorer">
    Souschef has a built-in Pinterest explorer that lets you browse and import recipes without ever leaving the app.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Tap the '+' button in Souschef">
        Open Souschef and tap the **\+** button at the bottom of your collection.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap the Pinterest logo">
        On the import screen, tap the **Pinterest** logo to open the in-app explorer.

        <img
          src="/images/screenshots/pinterest/explorer.webp"
          alt="Souschef import screen with the Pinterest explorer highlighted"
          className="help-screenshot"
        />
      </Step>
      <Step title="Browse and select a recipe">
        Browse recipes in the explorer, then select the one you want to import. Souschef will pull it into your collection.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Method 3: Paste a URL">
    Already have a Pinterest pin you want to save? You can paste the link directly into Souschef.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Copy the pin URL">
        In the Pinterest app or in your browser, open the pin and copy its URL.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open Souschef and tap '+'">
        Open Souschef and tap the **\+** button at the bottom of your collection.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Import from URL">
        Select **Import from URL** from the options.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Paste the URL and tap Import">
        Paste the pin URL and tap **Import**. Souschef will extract the recipe and add it to your collection.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
  Keep Souschef open while it processes the recipe — closing the app too early may interrupt the import.
</Tip>

## Why import Pinterest recipes into Souschef?

- **Turn inspiration into meals** — import recipes directly, then customize them, scale servings, and swap ingredients.
- **Smart shopping lists** — add all ingredients to your [shopping list](/using/shopping-list) in one tap, sorted by store section.
- **No more clutter** — keep everything in one place instead of juggling screenshots, links, and notes.
