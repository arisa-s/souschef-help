Importing from Instagram is included in the free version of Souschef.

Souschef makes it easy to save Instagram recipe posts and reels so you'll never lose track of a great dish again. When you import a post, Souschef extracts recipe information from the caption and, when available, the video's spoken audio — then organizes everything neatly in your recipe collection. There are two ways to do it, so pick whichever works best for you!

<Note>
  Souschef works best when the recipe is included in the post caption or spoken aloud in the video. If a post only shows the finished dish without explaining the recipe, Souschef may not have enough information to create a complete recipe.
</Note>

## How to import a recipe from Instagram

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Method 1: Share from Instagram">
    Already looking at a recipe in the Instagram app? Share it straight to Souschef.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Find a recipe and tap the send button">
        Browse Instagram and find a recipe post or reel you want to save. Tap the **Send** button (the paper airplane icon).

        <img
          src="/images/screenshots/instagram/step1.gif"
          alt="Instagram post with the Send button highlighted"
          className="help-screenshot"
        />
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Share to…">
        In the menu that appears, tap **Share to…** to open your phone's share sheet.

        <img
          src="/images/screenshots/instagram/step2.gif"
          alt="Instagram share menu with Share to highlighted"
          className="help-screenshot"
        />
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap the Souschef icon">
        Tap the **Souschef** icon in the share sheet. Souschef will open and start extracting the recipe automatically.

        <img
          src="/images/screenshots/instagram/step3.gif"
          alt="Share sheet with the Souschef app icon highlighted"
          className="help-screenshot"
        />

        Don't see Souschef in the share sheet? Follow [Add Souschef to your share menu](/getting-started/add-to-share-menu).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Wait while Souschef extracts the recipe">
        Souschef will extract the recipe from the post's caption and spoken audio when available. This usually takes up to 30 seconds, and then your recipe will be ready to view, edit, and cook!
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Method 2: Paste a URL">
    Already have an Instagram link you want to save? You can paste the URL directly into Souschef.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Copy the Instagram URL">
        In the Instagram app or in your browser, open the recipe post or reel and copy its URL.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open Souschef and tap '+'">
        Open Souschef and tap the **\+** button at the bottom of your collection.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap Import from URL">
        Select **Import from URL** from the options.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Paste the URL and tap Import">
        Paste the Instagram URL and tap **Import**. Souschef will extract the recipe and add it to your collection.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
  The process can take up to 30 seconds. Keep the Souschef app open while it works — closing the app too early may interrupt the import.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

If Souschef couldn't find a recipe, or the extracted details look incomplete, don't worry — it doesn't always mean something went wrong. See [Why a recipe import might fail](/importing/why-import-fails) for common reasons and easy fixes.
