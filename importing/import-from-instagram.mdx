Souschef makes it easy to save Instagram recipe posts and reels so you'll never lose track of a great dish again. When you share a post to Souschef, it analyzes the caption, comments, and any spoken audio in the video to extract the ingredients and cooking steps — then organizes everything neatly in your recipe collection.

<Note>
  Souschef works best with posts that include the recipe in the caption, comments, or audio (spoken in the video). If the post is just a beautifully filmed dish without any recipe details, Souschef may not be able to extract a complete recipe.
</Note>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait while Souschef extracts the recipe">
    Souschef will analyze the post's caption, comments, and audio to pull out the recipe. This usually takes up to 30 seconds, and then your recipe will be ready to view, edit, and cook!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The process can take up to 30 seconds. Keep the Souschef app open while it works — closing the app too early may interrupt the import.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

If Souschef couldn't find a recipe, or the extracted details look incomplete, don't worry — it doesn't always mean something went wrong. See [Why a recipe import might fail](/importing/why-import-fails) for common reasons and easy fixes.
