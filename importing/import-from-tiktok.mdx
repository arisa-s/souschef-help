Got a TikTok recipe you've been meaning to try? With Souschef, you can save it in just four taps and have a neatly organized recipe ready to cook — no more digging through your TikTok saved videos or re-watching a video just to remember the ingredients. Souschef scans the post's caption, comments, and spoken audio to build a complete, structured recipe.

<Note>
  Souschef supports TikTok posts that include the recipe in the caption, comments, or audio. If a video doesn't contain recipe details in any of those places, Souschef may not be able to extract a complete recipe.
</Note>

## How to import a recipe from TikTok

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a recipe and tap the ··· (Send to) button">
    Find a TikTok recipe post and tap the **···** (**Send to**) button.

    <img
      src="/images/screenshots/tiktok/step1.gif"
      alt="TikTok recipe post with the Send to button highlighted"
      className="help-screenshot"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Swipe through the social icons">
    Swipe through the row of social icons in the share menu.

    <img
      src="/images/screenshots/tiktok/step2.gif"
      alt="TikTok share menu showing social icons to swipe through"
      className="help-screenshot"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the ··· (More) button">
    Tap the **···** (**More**) button to open the full list of apps.

    <img
      src="/images/screenshots/tiktok/step3.gif"
      alt="TikTok share menu with the More button highlighted"
      className="help-screenshot"
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Souschef icon">
    Tap the **Souschef** app icon. Souschef will open and start extracting the recipe from the post's caption, comments, and audio.

    <img
      src="/images/screenshots/tiktok/step4.gif"
      alt="Share sheet with the Souschef app icon highlighted"
      className="help-screenshot"
    />

    Don't see Souschef? Follow [Add Souschef to your share menu](/getting-started/add-to-share-menu).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Stay in the app while Souschef processes the recipe — don't close it. The import can take up to 30 seconds, and then your recipe will be ready and waiting for you.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

If the import came back empty or the recipe details look off, there are a few common reasons why that can happen. Check out [Why a recipe import might fail](/importing/why-import-fails) for tips on what to do next.
