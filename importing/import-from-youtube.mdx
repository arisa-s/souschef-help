Ever wished you could cook along with a YouTube recipe without constantly pausing, rewinding, and squinting at the screen? Souschef uses AI to turn any YouTube cooking video into a fully structured recipe — complete with an ingredient list, step-by-step instructions with timestamps, and smart timers. Just share the video to Souschef and let it do the heavy lifting.

## What you get from a YouTube import

When you import a YouTube cooking video, Souschef reads the video title, description, comments, and the video itself to extract everything you need:

- **Ingredient list** — scalable to any serving size, with easy measurement conversions
- **Step-by-step instructions with video timestamps** — jump straight to any moment in the video without scrubbing around
- **Smart timers** — Souschef automatically detects timing steps so you can set a timer with a single tap
- **Shopping list ready in one tap** — add all ingredients to your shopping list, automatically organized by aisle
