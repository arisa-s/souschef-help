Souschef can pull recipes from almost anywhere you find food inspiration — whether you're scrolling through social media, watching a cooking video, browsing a recipe blog, or flipping through a printed cookbook. In most cases, it's just a tap or two away. Here's a quick overview of every source Souschef currently supports, with links to detailed how-to guides for each one.

## What about Facebook?

Facebook recipe support is on our roadmap! We're actively working on adding it. In the meantime, if you spot a recipe on Facebook, you can always take a screenshot and use the [Scan feature](/importing/import-from-photos) to import it.

## How importing works

Most sources work through your phone's **share sheet** — the menu that appears when you tap the Share button inside any app. Tap Share, find the Souschef icon, and the import kicks off automatically. If you don't see Souschef in your share sheet, check out [how to add Souschef to your share menu](/getting-started/add-to-share-menu).
