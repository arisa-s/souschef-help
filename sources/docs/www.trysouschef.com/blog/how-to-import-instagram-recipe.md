Tired of losing track of your favorite Instagram recipes? With Souschef, you can easily save and organize them in just a few taps! Follow these simple steps:

🐾 1:

Browse Instagram and find a recipe post or reel you want to save, and tap the **"⌲"** (send) button. (Souschef currently support post that has recipe in their caption, comments, or audio)

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/393c65fb0e7c0da270a116ec4fc0edfc1fbb0a22-1080x1920.gif)

🐾 2:

Select "Share to..." button

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/ba0af971c3f8e353b5179fd90b781782a2fbadca-1080x1920.gif)

🐾 3:

Tap the Souschef App Icon. Don’t see the icon? [Follow this guide](https://www.trysouschef.com/blog/how-to-import-instagram-recipe#) to add the Souschef shortcut.

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/4679bc52a4c8ec06fb771b7562667180a2b55430-1080x1920.gif)
