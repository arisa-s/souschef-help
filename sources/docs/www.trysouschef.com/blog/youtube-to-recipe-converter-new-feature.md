Are you sick of juggling between play and pause while trying to whip up something yummy from a YouTube recipe video? Well, me too! And I’ve been cooking up something for you…(🥁🥁)

Today, I’m excited to unveil a feature that's quickly become my personal favorite: Youtube Recipe Extractor.

Unlike basic transcription generators, Souschef leverages state-of-the-art AI technology to intelligently extract all the juicy recipe info directly from YouTube videos. We take everything — the title, description, those endless comments, and the video itself — and feed it to our sophisticated AI, which is designed to meticulously pull out the essential recipe details that you need.

### **Here’s What You’ll Get:**

**🥕 Ingredient Lists 🥕**

Need to scale a recipe up or down? Need to use grams instead of pounds? We’ve got you covered. Convert measurements on the fly and customize servings with just a few taps.

**🕰️ Step-by-Step Instructions with Timestamps 🕰️**

Get timestamped instructions so you can jump straight to the cooking action. Say goodbye to scrubbing back and forth.

#### **Of course, you’ll get standard Souschef features like:**

- **Grocery Lists:** Quickly generate a shopping list from your recipe (automatically organized by aisle or recipe), and share it with friends or family.
- **Smart Timers:** Encounter a step that requires timing? Set a smart timer with one tap.
- **Undisrupted Cooking:** Keep your phone unlocked while cooking.
- **Bookmarks:** Digging the dish? Bookmark it directly within the app to easily access it later.

… and more to come 🤫

### **Supporting YouTube Creators**

We respect the hard work of YouTube recipe creators. While Souschef extracts recipe information, it plays the original video in the background whenever a user is cooking (over and over). This means every view counts, supporting creators while providing an enhanced cooking experience for users.
