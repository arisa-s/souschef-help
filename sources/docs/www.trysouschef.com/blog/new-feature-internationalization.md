こんにちは！We’re thrilled to unveil a major update to the Souschef app: **Japanese language support is now live ！**

( ꈍᴗꈍ)🍡🍡

This exciting enhancement marks just the beginning of our mission to make Souschef a truly global culinary companion. With a fully localized Japanese interface and the ability to import recipes directly from Japanese sources, we’re breaking down language barriers and making cooking with Souschef more inclusive than ever.

But that’s not all—we’re already planning to add even more languages in the near future! 🤫

Here’s a quick overview of what’s new:

1. **Japanese Language Interface:** Navigate the entire app effortlessly in Japanese. From menus and navigation to help guides, every aspect of Souschef is now fully available in Japanese.
2. **Import Japanese Recipes:** Easily import recipes from Japanese cooking videos and websites directly into your Souschef collection. Expand your culinary repertoire with authentic Japanese dishes. 

### **What’s Next?**

As the creator of Souschef, my passion for cooking and solving personal culinary challenges drives every update. Born and raised in Japan, I have a deep love for Japanese cuisine and a mission to share its rich flavors with the world. While Japanese food is beloved globally, home cooking offers a unique and heartfelt experience that’s often overlooked in favor of restaurant fare. 

I rarely find my favorite dishes that my grandma made growing up on typical Japanese restaurant menus. While there are plenty of great Japanese home recipes online, language barriers and the need for local ingredients can make them daunting for a global audience. This challenge isn’t unique to Japan—home dishes from any country can be hard to access internationally. 

That’s why we’re tackling the first hurdle: the **language barrier**. By making Japanese recipes accessible in multiple languages, we’re lowering the entry point and inviting everyone to explore and enjoy authentic Japanese home cooking. And the best part? Our **recipe translation feature is already in development!**
