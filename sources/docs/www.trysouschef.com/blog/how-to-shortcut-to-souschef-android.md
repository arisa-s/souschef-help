To add Souschef as a favorited button in your sharing options, follow the simple instructions below.

(If you're iOS user, [refer to this blog](https://www.trysouschef.com/blog/how-to-shortcut-to-souschef))

Step 1:

#### Go to a browser app and tap on the 'Share' button.

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/0aa3c44ade9af2fd4ab37e6469e811d624d2112f-1076x95.png) 

Step 2:

#### Scroll to the bottom and long press on 'Souschef'.

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/3b3b1653524ef5a980d0881fef40cf2e6cb0b656-1076x950.png) 

**Step 3:**

#### Tap 'Pin Souschef'.

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/e244a114d7e9d076cbd226df8c2aecfcb6805c47-1076x381.png) 

🥳🥳 aaand voila - you should see Souschef shortcut as one of the first option when you try to share recipe from external app! 🥳🥳
