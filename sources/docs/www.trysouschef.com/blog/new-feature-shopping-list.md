We are excited to unveil the latest feature of the Souschef app: the Smart Shopping List 🗒️🧺₊˚⊹♡

Designed to make grocery shopping effortless, this new feature ensures you get everything you need for your meals—**smartly organized by store section** to save you time. Whether it’s dairy, produce, or spices, your list automatically sorts items by location, so you can breeze through the aisles without backtracking.

### **How It Works:**

> [View post on Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/reel/C6B3aE7r5AX/)

1. **Import a recipe** into Souschef app
2. Tap **“Add to Shopping List”**
3. **Adjust the serving size**, then raid your fridge and pantry to check off what you already have
4. Hit **“Add X items”**— and voila! Your list is now automatically sorted by aisle
5. **Share** the list to friends and family via text if they go shopping.

#### **Making more than one recipe?**

No problem! Add **multiple** recipes at once, and we’ll merge the ingredients for you

#### **Need extra stuff?**

You’re not just shopping for dinner—so go ahead and add things like diapers, toilet paper, or anything else your household needs

### **Get Started Today!**

Say goodbye to disorganized grocery lists and repeated trips back through the aisles. Update your Souschef app to the latest version and start building your Smart Shopping List today. Experience the joy of efficient, organized grocery shopping!
