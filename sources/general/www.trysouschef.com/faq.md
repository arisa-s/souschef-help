# Souschef FAQs

## Where can I import recipes from?

At this stage, Souschef can import recipes from almost any recipe blog, as well as Youtube and Pinterest, Instagram, and Tiktok. We are working to add support for Facebook!

## Is Souschef available for both iOS and Android devices?

Yes! - You can find Souschef in the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

## Can I use Souschef for free?

Souschef is free to download from the Apple App Store and Google Play. All features are free at this time! (subject to change in future)
