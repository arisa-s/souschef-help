# Shortcut (iOS)

Import even faster by adding the Souschef shortcut.

To add Souschef as a favorited button in your sharing options, follow the simple instructions below.

(If you're Android user, please refer to [this blog](https://www.trysouschef.com/blog/how-to-shortcut-to-souschef-android))

#### Step 1: Go to a browser app and tap on the 'Share' button.

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/3f50ff5239c7634a17dfbb4106510e77fa2d4cc0-1076x188.png) 

#### Step 2: Scroll to the right and tap 'More'.

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/86677f4b886c293e637e5868bb801ebdb49dd697-1076x443.png)

#### **Step 3:** Tap 'edit'.

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/e286f291b9317d8dbd3a5bc5d04ceb30688a7343-1076x450.png)

#### **Step 4:** Scroll to Souschef & tap the plus button.

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/3d4997a7e2c3defe0fec253fc22c5e5cd69fec85-1076x414.png) 

_Note: if the Souschef is not showing as an option, try restarting your phone - this typically ‘refreshes’ your share options._

#### **Step 5:** Drag Souschef to the top.

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/6a0bb3715a35db2886bce880c3c7a8fb64e86916-1076x546.png) 

aaand voila - you should see Souschef shortcut as one of the first option when you try to share recipe from external app!
