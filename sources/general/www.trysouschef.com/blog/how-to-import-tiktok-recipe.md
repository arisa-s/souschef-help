# Import Tiktok Recipe

Easily import and organize recipe posts on Tiktok

Arisa Shiraishi

Tired of losing track of your favorite TikTok recipes? With **Souschef**, you can easily import and organize them in just a few taps! Follow these simple steps:

🐾 Step 1:

Find a TikTok recipe post and tap the **"···" (Send to)** button. _(Souschef currently supports posts with recipes in the caption, comments, or audio.)_

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/af044cbba3f94e3ea3ccac50f94299fae891000e-1080x1920.gif)

🐾 Step 2:

Swipe through the social icons.

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/2001c1dcb439e4813b64c9eecb6ec590739c2ab3-1080x1920.gif)

🐾 Step 3:

Tap the **"···" (More)** button.

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/eb12a3ad35512f772cb3fda3d74bf3bf57646bc4-1080x1920.gif)

🐾 Step 4:

Select the **Souschef** app icon. _(Don’t see it? [Follow this guide](https://www.trysouschef.com/blog/how-to-import-tiktok-recipe#) to add the Souschef shortcut!)_

![](https://cdn.sanity.io/images/10y6s55e/production/4c4340bfb8b0236bf00cf55696da6dd87a7e925d-1080x1920.gif)

Now, sit back while Souschef analyzes the post’s **caption, comments, and audio** to extract the recipe! This process may take up to **30 seconds**, and voilà—your recipe is ready to cook! **૮₍ •⤙•˶₊˚ෆ**
